Since AMAGI has built a reputation for quality and being different, 'great' design is a core value to every experience we create.

Experience:

Newbie to Expert

Requirements:

  • Ability to demonstrate current skills with a portfolio
  • Experience with Adobe Creative Cloud applications
  • Demonstrated empathy to understand perspective of others
  • Be interested in working in a highly collaborative way where the expectations for every person are high
  • Constant desire to grow and develop your own abilities
